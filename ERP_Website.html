Kostal Dive Travel & Tours

Final Project – ERP Proposal and Presentation

A presentation by: Joshua Arnel A. Gonzales - Mapúa Malayan Colleges Mindanao

Business Overview

Kostal Dive Travel and Tours is a boat renting and Island Hopping business where water sports enthusiasts and partygoers may rent the equipment and services offered by the business so they may enjoy and wind-off on their oceanic travels. The services and paraphernalia offered by the business mainly extend to Scuba Diving and Island Hopping.

Project Description

This final project for Kostal Dive Travel & Tours builds upon a previously implemented system for voucher and permit creation, expanding into broader operational support through ERP. The ERP system developed focuses on three main modules: Customer Data Management, Boat Trip Scheduling, and Internal Communication between employees, coordinators, and the owner. The ERP system was developed to enhance operational efficiency and reduce reliance on manual processes.

The ERP brochure provided below outlines the purpose and design of the solution, highlighting key features such as centralized customer records, real-time trip scheduling, and integrated team chat. These features align closely with the business’s current needs and were selected to ensure minimal disruption to existing financial processes, which remain separate.

Chosen ERP

Odoo is a modular open-source ERP system designed to streamline business operations. For Kostal Dive Travel & Tours, Odoo fits perfectly by replacing our manual processes of cross-referencing and retrieval of old customer data with digitized records and real-time data access.

Why Odoo?

- It’s flexible and scalable for small business needs
- Modules can be activated individually
- Supports digital record-keeping, information retrieval, and document organization

Modules Used

Guest Information Database

Stores data for repeat customers to allow centralized and consistent recordkeeping of guest information.

Booking and Island-Hopping Scheduling

Allows real-time managing of booking dates and cargo delivery commissions. Supports correspondence between all employees in terms of important dates and schedules.

Live Chat

Allows all employees and coordinators to easily and freely communicate in real-time. Supports teamwork and communication between employees and prevents confusion when working together in bookings.

Benefits and Goals Aligned

The ERP system developed for Kostal Dive Travel & Tours directly aligns with the original project goals of improving operational efficiency, reducing manual errors, and enhancing coordination. By centralizing customer data, digitizing boat trip scheduling, and enabling real-time internal communication, the system streamlines essential business processes without disrupting existing workflows. It eliminates the need for scattered spreadsheets and messaging apps, replacing them with a unified interface that supports staff coordination and decision-making. The solution is scalable, user-friendly, and tailored to the company's workflow, ensuring it meets current operational demands while supporting future growth.
